### What is Nstbrowser?

Nstbrowser is an **[antidetect browser](https://hidemyacc.com/)** designed to help users manage multiple online accounts safely. It allows you to create isolated browser profiles, each with its own cookies, cache, and browser fingerprint, reducing the risk of account linking.

### What is Nstbrowser mainly used for?

Nstbrowser is commonly used for:
- Managing multiple social media accounts  
- Affiliate marketing and traffic arbitrage  
- E-commerce and marketplace operations  
- Automation and account testing  

Its primary goal is to keep each account running in a separate browser environment.

### How does Nstbrowser work?

Nstbrowser creates independent browser profiles based on Chromium. Each profile has unique fingerprint parameters such as user agent, timezone, language, canvas, WebGL, and proxy configuration, making every profile appear as a different device.

### Does Nstbrowser support multiple browsers?

No. Nstbrowser only supports **Chromium-based browsers**. Users who need Firefox, Safari-like, or other browser engines may find this limiting.

### Does Nstbrowser support mobile fingerprints?

No. Nstbrowser is mainly focused on **desktop browser fingerprints** and does not provide real Android or iOS fingerprint simulation.

### Does Nstbrowser include built-in proxies?

No. Nstbrowser does not offer built-in proxies. Users must connect their own residential, mobile, or datacenter proxies manually.

### Is Nstbrowser free?

Nstbrowser offers a **free plan with limited features**, suitable for testing. Paid plans unlock more browser profiles and higher usage limits for users who need to scale.

### Is Nstbrowser easy to use?

### Does Nstbrowser support automation?

Yes, but mainly through **API-based automation**. This makes Nstbrowser more suitable for developers and technical users rather than beginners looking for no-code automation.
