**1. What is GoLogin and what is it used for?**

GoLogin is an [antidetect browser](https://hidemyacc.com/) that allows users to manage multiple browser profiles on a single device while keeping each profile completely isolated. Every profile has its own digital fingerprint — including browser, OS, screen resolution, fonts, WebGL, and proxy setup - making them appear as separate real devices. This tool is commonly used by marketers, e-commerce sellers, and agencies to manage multiple accounts without being detected by platforms like Facebook, Google, or TikTok.

**2. How does GoLogin help avoid account bans or linking?**

Each GoLogin profile creates a unique environment. It prevents websites from identifying that the same person is controlling multiple accounts by randomizing fingerprint parameters. When paired with high-quality proxies, GoLogin makes each session look like it’s coming from a different real user. This reduces the risk of mass bans due to IP or browser fingerprint similarity.

**4. What are the main weaknesses or limitations of GoLogin?**

Despite its strengths, GoLogin has some limitations:
- Limited fingerprint customization: Compared to tools like Hidemyacc, it gives less control over certain fingerprint parameters (e.g., fonts, hardware details).
- No built-in automation tool: Automation requires third-party integration or API setup, which can be complex for non-developers.
- Proxy dependency: Its effectiveness depends heavily on the quality of proxies used. Free or unstable proxies can still lead to bans.
- Higher price at scale: Costs can rise quickly when managing hundreds of profiles.
  
These make GoLogin less ideal for users who want full automation or heavy-scale account operations at a low cost.
